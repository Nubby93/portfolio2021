Meet Iulian

Hello! I am a Web Designer at Pirelli. I work on the Pirelli Calendar platform and the newsletter campaigns related to the F1 races among other things.

Previously, I had a summer internship at a recruiting agency where I held interviews on behalf of brands such as Ford Motor Company and another summer internship at a construction company for which I created marketing materials.

In my free time I volunteer as a UI Designer at TheLastKnights, a community backed Social Strategy Game. Since 2019 I have created several banners and at least 180 icons.
